Medminder

Co-founder, 2018 UC Davis Hackathon
Davis, CA

Medminder is an app built on 2018 at UC Davis hackathon using swift with Apple’s IDE, XCode. Our goal is to provide an elderly-friendly pill management app that reminds the user to take their medications with a series of visual and auditory alerts. The reminders occur in intervals that the user specifies.
